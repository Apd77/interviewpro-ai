It calculates the percentage of time you maintain appropriate eye contact and provides specific feedback on when to look at the camera versus when brief glances away are natural.

  • Real-time gaze direction analysis
  • Natural vs. excessive looking away detection
  • Cultural context awareness for eye contact norms
  • Confidence correlation metrics
